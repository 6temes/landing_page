You are in good hands
Kiwi Kitchen's lunch services incorporate preparation and presentation of finger food and meals for businesses and schools, office functions and meetings, parties, BBQs and other functions, and production companies requiring food delivered on-site.

Please contact us to inquire about availability for your specific date and to discuss numbers, budget, food and other requirements. Note that we may not be able to cater for functions with very small numbers.
