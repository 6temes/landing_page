You are in good hands

Kiwi Kitchen will make your party or gathering one to remember by providing healthy and delicious food from all corners of the world.

We have been catering for Tokyo's international and Japanese
community since our establishment in 1999. Our cliental include embassys, large corporate banks and law firms as well as international schools and private homes.

Simply view our finger food or buffet menus  and  make your selection or contact us directly and we will put a menu proposal together to suit your needs. Kiwi Kitchen catering staff can also be arranged (with advance notice) for hire to serve food and drinks at various functions and events.

Ingredients Source list

Dear Valued Customers,

With the current situation of foods from the Tohoku and surrounding areas being suspected of contamination, Kiwi Kitchen is not using anything from those areas and has made all of our suppliers aware that we no longer accept any products that are even a possible risk. We have taken on the additional cost of sourcing a lot of international ingredients in order to be sure that our menu’s remain safe and healthy for you all to enjoy.
