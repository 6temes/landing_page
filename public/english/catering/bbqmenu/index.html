Buffet Menu

This menu is priced on the number of people attending your party or event, please see below for prices and menu options.

For parties of 30-60 this menu is ¥3,000 plus tax per person
For parties of 15-30 this menu is ¥4,000 plus tax per person
Please choose three mains, three salads and one dessert

If you would like to cater for more than 60 people or need to work within a budget please contact us to discuss menu options.


Please provide full details for your catering order, such as menu, number of guests, and delivery address at least one week before the event.


Our cancellation policy is as follows:
- Orders cancelled between 72 and 48 hours of the event will incur a charge of 20% of the total fee
- Orders cancelled between 48 and 24 hours of the event will incur a charge of 50% of the total fee
- Orders cancelled within 24 hours of the event will incur a charge of 100% of the total fee
- Orders reduced by more than 25% between 72 and 48 hours of the event will incur a charge of 20% of the reduced amount
- Orders reduced by more than 25% between 48 and 24 hours of the event will incur a charge of 50% of the reduced amount
- We cannot accept reductions within 24 hours of the event
