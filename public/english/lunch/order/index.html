Lunch with Kiwi Kitchen

To order simply click on the "Our Menu" button and choose from the latest selection - Our lunch menu changes everyday and we upload the next days selection each day around 14:00 each day.

Meals are generally delivered between 10:30am-12pm. If you have a meeting/function for which you require meals delivered, please contact Kiwi Kitchen before 9am on the same day. For orders for 10 people or more, please order at least one day prior.

If you would like to order for a future event e-mail us for a menu proposal at inquiries@kiwikitchen.com
