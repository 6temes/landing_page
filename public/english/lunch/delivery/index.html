Lunch with Kiwi Kitchen
Kiwi Kitchen delivers lunch to central Tokyo. Delivering through out Minato-ku and adjacent wards. Please contact us if you don’t see your area within our orange delivery zone on the map.

Deliver fees apply for all areas on weekends and public holidays and may apply any day for areas outside our regular delivery zone
