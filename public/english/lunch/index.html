Lunch with Kiwi Kitchen

Kiwi Kitchen's lunch delivery service incorporates delivery of tasty,
healthy lunches to businesses and schools within Metropolitan Tokyo from Monday through Friday.

From salads, sandwiches, wraps, fresh fruit salads and cakes to

a daily selection of hot dishes such as pastas, roasts, stir-fries and curries, pies and quiches, Kiwi Kitchen offers a vast array

of delicious meals to choose from.

Meals are generally delivered between 10:30am-1pm. If you have a meeting/function for which you require meals delivered or would like to join our regular delivery service, please contact Kiwi Kitchen by phone or e-mail.
