Leave it to Kiwi Kitchen ...
Kiwi Kitchen Home Packs are freshly made to order, and delivered chilled, ready for the fridge or freezer.
  • Please allow at least 3 WEEKDAYS (Monday to Friday) after we receive your order - delivery will be between 12:00 and 16:00 Monday to Friday.
  • Minimum order is 5,000 yen.
  • Free delivery within Minato-ku and surrounding areas for orders over 10,000 yen.
  • For smaller orders, delivery is 500 yen within Minato-ku and nearby areas.
