Easy to order...

Simply enter the number of items next to the size of the menu items that you wish to order; check your total order and click the "Submit your order" button to move to the next page that will summarize your order and ask you to fill in your name and delivery details; finally click the "Submit order" button and the system will automatically send you an order confirmation e-mail (please be sure to enter you correct e-mail address and contact details).

Orders for this service must be placed at least three working days in advance to guarantee availability; next day deliveries are sometime available if we are already making orders for the items you want but you must contact directly to request & confirm them.

We generally deliver Monday - Friday around 6pm but can deliver after 6pm, but on holidays & weekends we have an additional delivery fee if you are not able to receive our order at the regular times.

Please send any question to inquiries@kiwikitchen.com or call our kitchen directly 03-3442-2210. Also note that although we do deliver on weekends and public holidays we run our business Monday - Friday so any enquiries made from after 6pm on Friday and over the weekend will be responded to on the following Monday.
