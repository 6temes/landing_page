Leave it to us

Too busy or tired to cook each night? Fancy a wholesome home cooked meal, ready to heat and eat, when you get home?

Kiwi Kitchen's gourmet Home Pack service incorporates delivery of pre-ordered meal packs to customers within Metropolitan Tokyo (see our delivery area map).

Oven baked Meals and Pies are delivered in aluminum containers, ready for you to pop into the oven, and sauces come in microwavable containers. Moreover, all meals can come with separate, accompanying side dishes such as rice, pasta, bread rolls and sauces.

Kiwi Kitchen offers a vast array of delicious meals to choose from that are both nutritious and delicious (and with no artificial preservatives) . Home cooked in our kitchen, ready to be delivered to yours.
